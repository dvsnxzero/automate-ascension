If you don't have thinkorswim, this lesson is for you!

While we use thinkorswim to teach this course, we know many of our students use other platforms. Thinkorswim is not a requirement to trade and all concepts in this course (aside from thinkorswim's "on-demand" trading) are applicable to 90% of brokers outside of thinkorswim. Here are our tutorials on how to setup your platform in our style using a different brokerage. (Please see "broker secrets: how to choose a broker" for our suggestions on platforms for you to choose from).

- Interactive Brokers
- Webull
- Trading212

If your brokerage is not on the list, here are the specifications that we use:

1. SimpleMovingAvg(SMA): 9
2. SimpleMovingAvg(SMA): 180
3. RelativeStrengthIndex(RSI): 14 Days
4. MACD: Default

P.S. If your broker isn't on here and you need help adapting to the specifications, please tag @zipcharlie within our Private Tutoring Chat! (Also attach pictures so he knows what to work with)
