When it comes to brokers, make sure that you are getting a good deal. There is only one party that wins EVERY time you make a trade - and that is your broker. Making sure that you keep your trading expenses as low as possible is simply a characteristic of any smart businessperson.

## Why is this important?

Trading fees add up! They make it harder to become profitable and eat away at your returns.

If you make 250 trades a month at $6 a trade - that's an extra $1500 taken away from your trading account right off the top.

## How Do You Do Lower Fees?

Well, the first way to is to message your broker and ask them to lower it. Most people never ask, and thus brokerages like TD Ameritrade (before they went commission free) used to lower your fees by just simply asking. It used to be that you could only trade commission free by using apps like Robinhood. This often resulted in professional traders struggling with slow performance and a lack of sophisticated tools.

However, in late 2019, most major brokers eliminated commissions on stocks and ETFs.
