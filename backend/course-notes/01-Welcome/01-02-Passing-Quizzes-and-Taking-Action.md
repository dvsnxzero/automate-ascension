# Passing Quizzes & Taking Action

**Module:** Welcome!  
**Lesson:** 2 of 5

---

Throughout this course, you are going to be confronted with assessments and prompts. We recommend that you answer the assessments after you finish the modules & complete the prompts as you go through the lessons. This is something that we do NOT JUST to test your knowledge and attention, but also to reinforce concepts that you didn't understand prior to taking the quizzes. If you do not know something, write it down and go back and see if you can find the answer. It is extremely important to digest each concept, but do not stress yourself out if you miss some small details. At the end of the day, a lot of ideas will connect and click as you go through the course and apply concepts to the market.
