## What is Delisting?

Delisting is when a stock is subject to being removed from an exchange. This can be for a number of reasons, but the most common reason is falling below a certain threshold (falls below $1 a share, etc). This is almost always a terrible thing as stocks that are removed from an exchange are likely to get beat down afterwards.

## How can you avoid this?

Look up stocks that have received suspension or delisting. For example, NASDAQ has this list.

Also, be wary of stocks that are on the list for delisting. The company may be inclined to take drastic measures to bolster their share price to avoid getting delisted. This could be a reverse stock split, a merger, or a sketchy pumping news announcement.
