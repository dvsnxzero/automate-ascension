**Module:** Beginning Chart Analysis  
**Lesson:** 3 of 5

In this lesson, we will be diving into volume. As you go through it, ask yourself if you really understand what volume is - and then go and look at some charts outside of this and identify differences of volume and their interactions with the price action.

In this lesson, we will be diving into float. As you watch this, carefully follow and then proceed to look up different stocks and find their float. See if you can spot the different between some low float stocks, and some high float stocks. Which would you prefer to trade?

**Prompt:** Go analyze AAPL, CARA, DIS, CHK, and BABA. Which was has the highest volume? Which one has the lowest volume? Google their float. How do they compare?
