# Let's Trade! (Three DIFFERENT Scenarios)

**Module:** ETF Toolbox  
**Lesson:** 4 of 4

---

In this lesson Charlie is going to walk you through three examples of attacking different scenarios. Take note that the difficulties in these lessons display failures that are either the market's fault or are Charlie's fault. Some failures were due to a bad market setup, whereas others were due to bad trading on the part of Charlie. But all scenarios can be victorious if approached with the right risk management and skillset.

**Good Scenario & Good Trading:** Charlie dives into UVXY on a day with a clean run in implied volatility (which makes UVXY go up).

**Bad Scenario but Good Trading:** Charlie dives in SPXL/SPXS on a day with uncertain price strength, uncertain direction, and a lot of fake outs.

**Good Scenario but BAD Trading:** Charlie dives into LABU/LABD, doesn't perform perfectly (still grabs most of the move), and walks away with some insights.
