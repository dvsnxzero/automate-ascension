# Focus Of Indicators: Putting Odds In Your Favor

**Module:** Making Trades Using Indicators & Patterns  
**Lesson:** 1 of 15

---

In this Lesson, Charlie explains and introduces how to use Indicators and Studies to increase our Odds of Success.

Trading is at its core nothing but an odds game. Nothing is ever guaranteed in the stock market - and thus trading is all about keeping the odds of success in your favor.

It is really important to focus on this from the get-go because even in a setup that has a 80% success rate, it will still fail 20% of the time. This is assuming that you are choosing high quality setups (which most folks don't). If you look at the low-probability setups that most take - and then combine that with lack of risk management and inaccurate probability calculations - the failure rate is no surprise.

To become a victor in this game of probabilities, we must focus on two things.

1.) Positions That Have A High Probability Of Success

By Identifying: Support & Resistance, Upward Potential, Downward Potential, Previous Patterns, Confirmation, Strength, Specific Execution Points, Indications
