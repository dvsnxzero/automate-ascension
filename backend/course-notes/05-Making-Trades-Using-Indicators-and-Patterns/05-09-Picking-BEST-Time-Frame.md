# Picking BEST Time Frame

**Module:** Making Trades Using Indicators & Patterns  
**Lesson:** 9 of 15

---

Picking the BEST time chart is not only important, but it absolutely essential to your success as a trader.

**Take Away:** FOCUS on the time chart that corresponds to the duration of your position, while in the position. However, take into consideration the other time charts when planning and evaluating your position.
