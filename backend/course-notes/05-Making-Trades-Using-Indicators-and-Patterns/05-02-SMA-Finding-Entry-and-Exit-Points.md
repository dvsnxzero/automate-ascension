# SMA: Finding Entry & Exit Points

**Module:** Making Trades Using Indicators & Patterns  
**Lesson:** 2 of 15

---

The SMA (Simple Moving Average) is an extremely useful indicator that allows us to find entry & exit points while also allowing us to measure both price strength & directional strength.

What Is A CONFIRMATION point? What is a VALIDATION point?

## Simple Moving Average Recommended Specifications

- Long Term SMA (Directional Strength): (Close,180,0,no)
- Short Term SMA (Price Strength): (Close,9,0,no)
